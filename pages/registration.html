Registration Fees and Policies

  1. Per IEEE policy, for each accepted paper to be published in the proceedings, at least one author is required to register at the FULL AUTHOR REGISTRATION RATE.
  2. Registration covers: proceedings, coffee breaks, lunches, reception, gala dinner, and all sessions.
  3. Main conference papers require a full registration to be included in the proceedings.
  4. Fees must be paid prior to uploading the final camera-ready version.

IEEE STUDENT REGISTRATION

To qualify for the IEEE student reduced registration fee, you must hold an active IEEE student membership.

INVITATION LETTER

Letters of invitation will be provided only to authors with an accepted paper and/or those who have paid the full registration fee.

To request a letter of invitation for visa purposes, please contact: info@comcomap.net.

WIRE TRANSFER REGISTRATION

If you would like to pay your registration fees through wire transfer, please contact the conference organizers at: info@commantel.net. In your email, please indicate the EDAS paper number(s) (10 digits) and title(s).

CANCELLATION POLICY

All refund/cancellation requests must be received by October 30th, 2025. An administrative fee of 10% of the registration fee will be deducted from each refund.
