Call for Papers

The Computing, Management, and Telecommunications Conference (ComManTel 2025) invites researchers, practitioners, and academicians to submit original technical papers for presentation. ComManTel 2025 provides an international forum to explore the latest advancements, challenges, and applications at the nexus of digital technology, organizational strategy, and modern communication systems.

Submission Guidelines

Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers of up to 6 pages in length. Papers can be extended to a maximum of 8 pages, with an additional charge of $100 per extra page beyond 6 pages. All submissions must adhere to the conference formatting guidelines and be submitted through the EDAS platform. Detailed submission instructions and templates are available on the conference website. High-quality papers will be invited for consideration in associated Journal Special Issues.

Topics of Interest

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Computing Technologies:
    • Parallel and distributed computing
    • High-performance computing
    • Grid and cloud computing
    • Green computing and sustainable IT
    • Artificial intelligence, machine learning, and generative AI
    • Knowledge discovery, data mining, and cybersecurity in computing
  • Information Technology Management:
    • Digital transformation and innovation management
    • Project and agile management
    • Information management and data governance
    • Enterprise and resource management
    • E-business, E-government, and business intelligence
  • Telecommunications Systems and Networks:
    • Next-generation wireless communications (5G/6G and beyond)
    • Cognitive radio and dynamic spectrum access
    • Software-defined networking (SDN) and network virtualization
    • IoT, sensor networks, and smart connectivity
    • Cybersecurity and performance analysis in telecommunications
  • Systems Engineering and Applications:
    • Robotics, automation, and control systems
    • Advanced manufacturing and smart production
    • Biomedical and biotechnology engineering systems
    • Sustainable infrastructure and environmental impact assessment

For further details and updates, please visit the official conference website.
