REGISTRATION

Camera Ready Guidelines

The below instructions are essential to ensure that you comply with the publication requirements set by IEEE. Accepted papers will appear in the ComManTel 2025 proceedings and will be submitted to IEEE Xplore for possible inclusion.

Note: At least one author must register and present the paper (onsite or online) to be included in the proceedings.

1. Revise Your Paper

Check the reviewers' feedback carefully and edit your paper accordingly.

Important: Remove all headers, footers, and page numbering from your final manuscript.

2. Use IEEE PDF Xpress

All final manuscripts must be generated using PDF Xpress to ensure compatibility:

  • Visit: https://ieee-pdf-express.org/
  • Conference ID: 68363X
  • Upload your source file and receive the certified PDF by email. Do not change the file name received.

3. Register Your Paper

Registration is handled through the PayQlick Platform. Select the "Author Registration" option. You can find rates at: Registration Page.

4. Submit IEEE Copyright Form (eCF)

  1. Log in to "View My Registration" at PayQlick.
  2. Click the Copyright Form button.
  3. Follow the IEEE instructions to complete the electronic submission.

5. Upload Camera-Ready Paper

  1. Return to "View My Registration" at PayQlick.
  2. Click Camera Ready (Must be the PDF certified by PDF Xpress).
  3. Upload the file before the deadline.

Issues and Support

If you have any questions or face any issues during your registration, PDF-Xpress, eCF, or camera-ready upload, please submit a support ticket at: Support
