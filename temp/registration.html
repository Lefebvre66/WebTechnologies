Registration Fees and Policies

  1. Per IEEE policy, for each accepted paper to be published in the IEEE COMCOMAP 2025 Proceedings and IEEE Xplore®, at least one author (including students) is required to register at the FULL AUTHOR REGISTRATION RATE. Moreover, the paper must be presented by an author named on the paper.
  2. Conference registration covers: proceedings, coffee breaks, lunches, reception, gala dinner, all co-located workshops, and attendance at all keynotes.
  3. Main conference papers require a full registration. Otherwise, the paper will be excluded from the proceedings.
  4. Non-refundable registration fees must be paid prior to uploading the final camera-ready version of the paper.
  5. The final version of accepted papers must follow the standard 2-column IEEE format.
  6. Authors must follow the allowed paper length. Extra pages can be purchased with an additional fee.

Wire Transfer Registration

If you would like to pay your registration fees through wire transfer, please contact the conference organizers at: info@comcomap.net. In your email, please indicate the EDAS paper number(s) (10 digits) and title(s).

Cancellation Policy

All refund/cancellation requests must be received by October 30th, 2025. An administrative fee of 10% of the registration fee will be deducted from each refund.
