Madrid Trivia & Curiosities

While attending ComManTel 2025, you might want to know these interesting facts about our host city:

  • Highest Capital: Madrid is the highest capital city in the European Union, located about 667 meters above sea level.
  • The Oldest Restaurant: According to the Guinness World Records, Sobrino de Botín (founded in 1725) is the oldest continuously operating restaurant in the world.
  • Sun Symbolism: The "Puerta del Sol" is the exact center (Km 0) of the Spanish radial road network.
  • Art Triangle: Madrid is home to the "Golden Triangle of Art," comprising the Prado, Reina Sofía, and Thyssen-Bornemisza museums, all within walking distance.
